'The Last Broadcast' Script

[INTRO] 0_1
"Pilot, this is Station Wren calling. Do you read me?
Enemy action has severed our wireless network across the southern line.
Six radio towers remain silent. Without them, our coastal stations are blind.
Your orders are simple, but vital. Reach each tower, clear the skies and restore the broadcast.
Once the signal is returned, command can warn the fleet before the enemy advance begins.
Time is not our ally, pilot. Every minute of silence places more lives in danger."

[ENEMY] 
E_1 “Enemy fighters approaching from above. Break their formation.”
E_2 “Enemy patrol closing fast. Do not let them drive you off course.”
E_3 “They are trying to keep the towers silent. Do not give them the satisfaction.”

[TOWER NOT RESTORED LINES]
T2_1 Stay with the route. Keep your nerve.
T2_2 That tower carried messages from the eastern watch posts. We have heard nothing from them since.
T2_3 Keep moving. You are carrying the only signal strong enough to restart the relay.

[TOWER RESTORED LINES]
T1_1 “That is it. The tower is transmitting again.”
T1_2 “Signal strength is weak, but holding. Well done.”
T1_3 “We are receiving fragments from the eastern posts. Not enough to act upon, but enough to know they are still there.”
T1_4 “The network is waking, pilot. Proceed to the next tower.”
T1_5 Signal restored. All towers are transmitting
